# Step 11 — UV Curing

> UV curing procedure for IP-S is identical to Workflow A. Expose at 405 nm for 10–20 minutes.

---

## Procedure

Follow the procedure in [Workflow A / step_11_uv_curing.md](../Workflow_A_63x_DiLL_IP_Dip2/step_11_uv_curing.md).

IP-S note: IP-S is already one of the more mechanically stable resists before UV post-cure. Post-curing is still recommended for complete crosslinking, especially for mechanical, optical, or biocompatibility applications.
