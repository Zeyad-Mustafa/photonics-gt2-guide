## Procedure

Follow the procedure in [Workflow A / step_07_load_sample_holder.md](../Workflow_A_63x_DiLL_IP_Dip2/step_07_load_sample_holder.md).

Key reminders specific to Workflow B:
- Confirm the ITO side of the substrate faces downward (toward the 25x objective)
- Hold the holder level when carrying it to the stage — IP-S is viscous but will still shift if the holder is tilted
- Verify in NanoWrite: Universal Sample Holder selected, 25x objective active
