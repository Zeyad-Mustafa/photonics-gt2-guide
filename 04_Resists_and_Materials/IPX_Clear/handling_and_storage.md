# IPX-Clear — Handling and Storage

---

## Storage Requirements

| Condition | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Temperature | 2-8 C |
| Light protection | Required |
| Shelf life (unopened) | ~12 months |
| Shelf life (opened) | Use within 6 months |

---

## Special Handling Note: Cleanliness

Because IPX-Clear is used for optical applications where clarity is critical, **cleanliness during dispensing is more important than with other resists**:

- Any dust, fiber, or contamination in the resist drop will be visible in transmitted light and will scatter light in the cured structure
- Work in a clean environment (clean bench or laminar flow hood if available)
- Use a fresh pipette tip for each dispense — do not reuse tips
- If particles are visible in the drop on the substrate, remove the drop, clean the substrate, and redispense
- Inspect the cured structure under transmitted light before committing to further processing

| Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Gloves | Nitrile |

## Waste Disposal

- Liquid IPX-Clear: acrylate/photopolymer chemical waste
- Developer waste: halogen-free organic solvent waste
