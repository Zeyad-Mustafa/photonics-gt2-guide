# IP-S — Handling and Storage

---

## Storage Requirements

| Condition | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Temperature | 2-8 C (refrigerator) |
| Light protection | Required — store in original dark vial or foil-wrapped |
| Shelf life (unopened) | ~12 months from manufacture date |
| Shelf life (opened) | Use within 6 months |
| Position | Upright, tightly sealed |

Allow IP-S to equilibrate to room temperature for 30 minutes before use. Cold resist has higher viscosity and may not wet the substrate correctly.

---

## Personal Protective Equipment

IP-S contains acrylate monomers and is a potential skin sensitizer with repeated exposure.

| PPE Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Gloves | Nitrile (not latex) |
| Eye protection | Safety glasses |
| Lab coat | Standard chemical-resistant |
| Work area | Fume hood recommended |

---

## Dispensing

- Use a clean dropper or micropipette tip
- Do not return unused resist to the stock vial
- Keep the vial capped at all times except when dispensing
- Work in subdued lighting or under yellow safe-light

---

## Waste Disposal

- Liquid IP-S: dispose as acrylate/photopolymer chemical waste
- Development waste (PGMEA + dissolved IP-S): halogen-free organic solvent waste
- Cured structures: solid chemical waste (confirm with EHS)

---

## Signs of Degradation

- Increased viscosity or gel formation
- Cloudiness or precipitate
- Dark yellow or brown discoloration
- Increased polymerization threshold vs. previous calibration

Discard and replace if any of these are observed.
