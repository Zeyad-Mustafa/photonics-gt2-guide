# IP-Visio — Handling and Storage

---

## Storage Requirements

Same as all IP-series resists:

| Condition | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Temperature | 2-8 C |
| Light protection | Required |
| Shelf life (unopened) | ~12 months |
| Shelf life (opened) | Use within 6 months |

---

## Additional Handling Notes for Biological Applications

Because IP-Visio is intended for biomedical use, additional handling considerations apply beyond standard chemical safety:

**Before use in cell culture or animal studies:**

- Treat IP-Visio like any other synthetic polymer that will contact biological tissue
- Complete full development (PGMEA + IPA) and UV curing before biological contact
- Sterilize printed structures before cell seeding: UV irradiation (30-60 min in biosafety cabinet) is the gentlest method and does not damage the structure
- Ethanol sterilization (70% EtOH, 10-15 min) is also compatible but rinse thoroughly with sterile PBS afterward
- Do not autoclave unless you have verified the structure survives autoclave temperatures (121 C steam) — small thin features may warp

**Biocompatibility is conditional:**

Uncured IP-Visio monomer is cytotoxic. The biocompatibility certification applies to fully cured and developed structures only. If structures are underdeveloped (residual monomer present), cell viability will be compromised. Develop thoroughly and cure fully before any biological use.

## PPE

| Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Gloves | Nitrile |
| Eye protection | Safety glasses |
| Lab coat | Standard |

---

## Waste Disposal

- Liquid IP-Visio: acrylate/photopolymer chemical waste (same as other IP-series)
- Do not dispose of biological samples that have contacted IP-Visio as standard chemical waste — follow your institution's rules for mixed biological/chemical waste
