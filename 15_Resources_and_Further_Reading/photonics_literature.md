# Photonics Literature

## Overview

This file covers published work on photonic devices fabricated by two-photon polymerization, including photonic crystals, optical waveguides, resonators, and plasmonic structures.

---

## Photonic Crystals

Deubel, M., Von Freymann, G., Wegener, M., Pereira, S., Busch, K., and Soukoulis, C. M.
Direct laser writing of three-dimensional photonic-crystal templates for telecommunications.
Nature Materials, 3, 444-447, 2004.
DOI: 10.1038/nmat1155

Demonstration of woodpile photonic crystal templates with a complete photonic bandgap at telecommunications wavelengths (1.3 um), fabricated by 2PP in SU-8. This paper established 2PP as the primary tool for 3D photonic crystal fabrication and is the most-cited paper in this application area.

---

Meza, L. R., Das, S., and Greer, J. R.
Strong, lightweight, and recoverable three-dimensional ceramic nanolattices.
Science, 345(6202), 1322-1326, 2014.
DOI: 10.1126/science.1255908

While focused on mechanical metamaterials rather than photonics, this paper demonstrated alumina hollow-tube lattices fabricated by 2PP + ALD coating + polymer removal, producing ceramic structures with extreme specific strength. The fabrication route (2PP polymer template + ALD + ashing) is directly applicable to making inorganic photonic crystal templates.

## Optical Waveguides

Gaso, P., Pudis, D., Kuzma, A., and Goraus, M.
Optical waveguides prepared by 3D direct laser writing.
Journal of Lightwave Technology, 38(16), 4519-4524, 2020.
DOI: 10.1109/JLT.2020.2991677

Systematic characterization of propagation loss in IP-Dip-based optical waveguides as a function of printing parameters. Measured losses of 1 to 5 dB/cm at 1550 nm. Provides the clearest experimental data for waveguide loss vs parameter trade-offs in 2PP-printed polymer waveguides.

---

Lindenmann, N., Balthasar, G., Hillerkuss, D., Schmogrow, R., Jordan, M., Leuthold, J., Freude, W., and Koos, C.
Photonic wire bonding: a novel concept for chip-scale interconnects.
Optics Express, 20(16), 17667-17677, 2012.
DOI: 10.1364/OE.20.017667

Introduced photonic wire bonding — using 2PP to print freeform waveguide bridges between photonic chips, enabling chip-to-chip optical coupling without precise mechanical alignment. This is one of the most practically significant photonics applications of 2PP and has been commercialized. Highly recommended reading for integrated photonics applications.

---

Lindenmann, N., Dottermusch, S., Goedecke, M. L., Hoose, T., Billah, M. R., Onanuga, T. N., Hofmann, A., Freude, W., and Koos, C.
Connecting silicon photonic circuits to multicore fibers by photonic wire bonding.
Journal of Lightwave Technology, 33(4), 755-760, 2015.
DOI: 10.1109/JLT.2014.2373051

Extension of photonic wire bonding to multi-core fiber coupling. Demonstrates the 3D routing capability of 2PP waveguides connecting a chip to each individual core of a multi-core fiber simultaneously.

## Optical Resonators and Cavities

Malinauskas, M., Farsari, M., Piskarskas, A., and Juodkazis, S.
Ultrafast laser nanostructuring of photopolymers: A decade of advances.
Physics Reports, 533(1), 1-31, 2013.
DOI: 10.1016/j.physrep.2013.07.005

Comprehensive review of ultrafast laser nanostructuring including resonator fabrication, covering the decade from approximately 2003 to 2013. Useful as a historical perspective and as a source for early resonator results.

## Plasmonic Structures and Templates

Radke, A., Gissibl, T., Klotzbucher, T., Braun, P. V., and Giessen, H.
Three-dimensional bichiral plasmonic crystals fabricated by direct laser writing and electroless silver plating.
Advanced Materials, 23(27), 3018-3021, 2011.
DOI: 10.1002/adma.201100543

Demonstrated 3D bichiral plasmonic crystals — arrays of interleaved left- and right-handed helical elements — fabricated by 2PP and silver-coated by electroless plating. Produced strong optical activity (circular dichroism) at visible wavelengths. A key paper for chiral plasmonic applications.

---

Gansel, J. K., Thiel, M., Rill, M. S., Decker, M., Bade, K., Saile, V., von Freymann, G., Linden, S., and Wegener, M.
Gold helix photonic metamaterial as broadband circular polarizer.
Science, 325(5947), 1513-1515, 2009.
DOI: 10.1126/science.1177031

This Science paper demonstrated a 3D gold helix photonic metamaterial — a 3D chiral metallic structure functioning as a broadband circular polarizer — fabricated by 2PP followed by electrodeposition of gold. One of the most influential papers in 3D photonic metamaterials. The fabrication route (2PP template + electrodeposition + polymer removal) is a standard approach for metallic 3D photonic structures.

## 3D Photonic Integration

Thiel, M., Fischer, J., von Freymann, G., and Wegener, M.
Direct laser writing of three-dimensional submicron structures using a continuous-wave laser at 532 nm.
Applied Physics Letters, 97(22), 221102, 2010.
DOI: 10.1063/1.3521464

Demonstrated 2PP with a continuous-wave laser rather than a pulsed laser, at 532 nm. Relevant for understanding the parameter space beyond standard femtosecond systems and for characterizing the minimum requirements for 2PP absorption.
